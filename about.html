About Me

I appreciate many aspects in my profession. I can definitely say that I found myself in terms of a career. I believe that the design gives the ideal balance between the arts and the business.

I have 5 years of experience in the design area and during that time I have thoroughly studied the trends, design tricks and everything that has already been done by many other really wonderful designers . Now I try to move on and away from the standards. I am bringing a bit more conceptual art into my new works, constantly looking for new visualizations and trying not to repeat the success of others, but to create my own. Thereby I offer more unique solution to my clients which will benefit to the delivery of business objectives.

I strongly believe that every strange and unusual visual solution can become a brand new trend.
